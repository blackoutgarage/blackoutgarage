FAQ

How long does tint take?

Most full cars are completed within 2–3 hours depending on size and film.

Is it legal?

We’ll guide you on state legal limits and offer options that stay compliant.

How do I care for new tint?

Don’t roll windows for 2–3 days. Clean with ammonia‑free glass cleaner and soft towels.

What is the warranty?

Lifetime coverage against bubbling, peeling, fading, or color change for original owner.
