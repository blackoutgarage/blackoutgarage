FAQ

How long does tint take?

Most cars are done in about 1–3 hours depending on size and film type. We’ll give you an estimated completion time when you drop your vehicle off and text you as soon as it’s ready.

My tint looks hazy or has small bubbles — is that normal?

This is completely normal. Small hazy spots or water pockets can appear as the film dries. They will disappear as the moisture evaporates, usually within a few days to two weeks depending on sunlight and temperature.

How do I care for new tint?

Keep your windows up for at least 3–5 days to allow the film to fully cure. We’ll confirm the ideal wait period when your tint is complete. Once cured, clean your windows only with an ammonia-free glass cleaner and soft towels to help maintain a clear, long-lasting finish.

Do you offer tint removal?

We can safely remove old or damaged window film using a controlled process that protects your glass and defroster lines. Tint removal does have an additional cost since it requires extra time and care before applying new film.
